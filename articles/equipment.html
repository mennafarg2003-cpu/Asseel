- 5 Star Equine Products Close Contact Barrel Racer

The 5 Star Close Contact Barrel Racer Pad is designed for comfort, protection, and high performance in barrel racing It reduces pressure on the withers, neck, and spine, allowing free shoulder movement and better performance The butterfly cutout design ensures closer contact between horse and rider for improved balance and control Handcrafted in the USA and fully customizable to fit individual needs.

- Cashel Company LG4 Crusader Leg Guards

The Cashel LG4 Crusader Leg Guards are designed to protect your horse’s lower legs from flies, biting insects, burrs, and stickers Made with durable, tear-resistant mesh that keeps its shape and stands upright for full coverage protection ,They offer a comfortable, non-binding fit suitable for both stalls and pastures Available in multiple colors and sizes, sold as a set of four.
